Our system is a health care system includes people, institutions, and resources that deliver health care services to patients in the best way with best performance. This system is based on smart cards that are used in information technologies as portable integrated devices with data storage. The system uses smart card for personal identification and transfer of health data and provides data communication between all users in the system.
